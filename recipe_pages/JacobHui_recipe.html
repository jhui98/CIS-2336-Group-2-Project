Chicken Adobo is actually the national dish in the Philippines. It is a very easy and rich dish that is simmered in a soy sauce and vinegar based marinade. Adobo can be made with with chicken or pork, but I chose chicken for this recipe as it's my favorite, and it is also traditionally served over rice to soak up the rich sauce that the chicken is also marinated in.
