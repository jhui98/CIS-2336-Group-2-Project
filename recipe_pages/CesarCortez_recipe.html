Homemade Bluberry Pancakes - Cesar Cortez

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of your choice of milk
  • 2 tbps of white vinegar
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbps of sugar (can add less or more)
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • Half a tsp of baking soda
  • Half a tsp of salt
  • 2 tbps of melted butter
  • 1 or more cup of blueberries (fresh recommended)
  • Extra butter to spread on the pan

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk and vinegar and allow it to sit four around two minutes
  2. Combine the dry ingredients together and whisk them. After, whisk the egg, milk, and butter into the dry ingredients until they combine. (Make sure to not overwhisk)
  3. Heat a preferrably nonstick pan, then melt a smear of butter to give the pancake a golden brown crust
  4. Pour a third of a cup of batter into the skillet, and spread it flat. Add some blueberries on top, and cook until bubbles start to build up. Flip and let it cook for another minute and a half - the pancakes should be fluffy and cooked. Repeat for more pancakes.
  5. Top with butter, syrup, and any other toppings. Grab one (or the whole stack) and enjoy your breakfast!
Pancakes are one of the fan favorites for breakfast around the world. But you know what's better thank plain old pancakes? Bluebbery Pancakes! Not only are these pancakes already fluffy, but the blueberries add an extra "tang" that you have been missing with normal pancakes. This recipe will guide you on how YOU can make your very own, cost-effective, homemade, fluffy pancakes with little to no hassle.
